Overview

Purpose

Rafting Rapids website is a site that will spread awareness of the best rapids to raft in your local area. We also will offer options to buy or rent equipment to be able to make revenue from this sight. Our goal is also to talk about how to get into rafting and how to start as a new person. We will also offer ways for the customers to get in contact with different rafting groups that will help newer people with rafting. Overall our goal is to get people into rafting and building a website that will help provide locations, equipment, and communities for people to use.

Audience

Our Audience is people who are looking into getting into rafting or already into rafting. They are people who like to thrill seeking activities and have enough income to rent and buy equipment for a new hobby that they would like to get into. They are people who are people who are from their teens all the way to retirement age. These people need a way to communicate with each other and plan groups to go rafting with. The audience will usually use a desktop or laptop to access the sight. They will come to this site because they want to get educated about white water rafting and how to get into the hobby. It will also provide a way for people who love rafting to talk with each other and get together to raft.
